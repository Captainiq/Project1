Learn about Nimbus

What is Nimbus?

Nimbus is a digital healthcare company that provides medical diagnosis and discreet, affordable pharmacy solutions to life's embarrassing (yet common) problems, for both men and women. Strut makes it easy to access these solutions when it’s convenient for you, and from the comfort of your home. We all deserve to live our best lives.

Why use Nimbus?

Nimbus, is a LegitScript accredited digital healthcare company. We are held to strict standards to ensure our U.S. licensed physicians treat you on a safe and secure PCI compliant website.

Are the medicines used genuine?

Yes absolutely. Strut uses medications from Verified-Accredited Wholesale Distributors (VAWD) and sources active prescription ingredients that are FDA approved.

What states do you service?

Why is Nimbus only available in certain state?

Due to telemedicine laws, a physician has to be licensed in the state where the patient is located. Strut’s physicians are licensed in only select states. We’re growing quickly, email care@strutyours.com so we can provide a solution and expedite treatment to your community.

How do I get started?

It’s super simple. First, log in. Then, fill out our medical questionnaire to help us understand you and your specific needs better. Our board-certified physicians will then review your answers and, upon approval, your medications will be discreetly delivered to your doorstep. Deliveries typically arrive within 2 business days once your physician has authorized your prescription.

Learn about Telehealth

Is telehealth legal and safe?

Yes. Nimbus’s innovative medical platform allows our licensed physicians to treat patients remotely and safely via secure chat, phone or video. Telemedicine laws allow physicians to practice medicine and prescribe medications online, when collecting accurate and truthful information from a patient. A physician will customize a treatment plan for each patient to ensure safety. The personalized treatment plan must be followed before taking any medication. A patient must adequately weigh the risks and benefits of the treatment plan after reviewing it entirely and fully understanding the content.

Can I speak to my doctor on the phone/video chat?

Yes, at any time. Physician messaging is available on the login page. To speak directly with a physician, contact our customer service team, and we’ll get it arranged right away. Just email care@nimbusmenscare.com or call (530) 625-8172.

Who are the doctors prescribing medications for Nimbus?

Nimbus utilizes a knowledgeable team of US-licensed and board-certified physicians.

Do I need to pick up my medication from a pharmacy?

Only if you would like to. After your evaluation with one of our board-certified physicians, your medications will arrive within about 2 business days. If you choose to pick up your prescription from your own pharmacy, your medication could be 3-4 times as expensive. Your pharmacy will also need to be part of the Surescript network of pharmacies. We use a preferred pharmacy network; Family Pharmacy and Keller Pharmacy.

What happens if there's an emergency?

Please call 911 immediately.
